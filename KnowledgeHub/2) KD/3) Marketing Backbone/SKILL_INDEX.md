Use this group when the task is about demand, conversion, positioning, activation, retention, or revenue communication.

This index is a router, not the skill content. Read it to select one exact subskill, then open only that subskill's `SKILL.md`.

## Load When

- The user asks for marketing, launch, growth, go-to-market, positioning, or product marketing.
- The task touches SEO, AI SEO, programmatic SEO, schema, site architecture, or directory submissions.
- The task involves landing-page copy, pricing-page copy, ads, social, email, SMS, lifecycle, onboarding, churn, referrals, or CRO.
- The user wants customer research, competitor research, market ideas, lead magnets, or sales collateral.

## Do Not Load When

- The task is implementation-only and has no go-to-market or conversion decision.
- The task is legal/compliance. Use `4) Legal`.
- The task is strategic company direction without a marketing execution surface. Use `6) Beirat`.
